WHERE:
M = Market charge

s = Number of days in current semi-annual period
i = Treasury borrowing rate over the remaining term to maturity, based on semi-annual interest payments and expressed in decimals
n = Number of remaining full semi-annual periods from the redemption date to the original maturity date, except that if the redemption date is on an interest payment date, n will be one less than the number of full semi-annual periods remaining to maturity
