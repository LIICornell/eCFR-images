Create a 24-month baseline and update as follows:

For reporting months 1-24...
Collect and test one sample each month. Each quarter, calculate a weighted average percentage of inherent moisture:
  • Multiply a month's inherent moisture percentage by tons produced or shipped. You now have the month's inherent moisture tonnage.
  • Add up 3 months of that inherent moisture tonnage.
  • Divide by tons produced or shipped in those 3 months.
Report the quarter's weighted average percentage on OSM-1.
