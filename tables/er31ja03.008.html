Municipal Waste Combustion Technology Limits for Class I Municipal Waste Combustion Units
1. Mass bum waterwall 200 parts per million by dry volume
2. Mass bum rotary waterwall 170 parts per million by dry volume
3. Refuse-derived fuel 250 parts per million by dry volume
4. Fluidized bed 220 parts per million by dry volume
5. Mass bum refractory 350 parts per million by dry volume
6. Modular excess air 190 parts per million by dry volume
7. Modular starved air 380 parts per million by dry volume
a Class I units mean small municipal waste combustion units subject to this subpart that are located at municipal waste combustion plants with an aggregate plant combustion capacity greater than 250 tons per day of municipal solid waste. See ยง62:15410 for definitions. b Nitrogen oxides limits are corrected to 7 percent oxygen, dry basis. c All limits are 24-hour daily block arithmetic average concentration. Compliance is determined for Class I units by continuous emission monitoring systems.
