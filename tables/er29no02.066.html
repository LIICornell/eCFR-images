(d) Loans Sold With Recourse
Amount of loans by recourse Alternative risk weighting
Recourse 6% or greater .06
Recourse <6% Weighted average recourse percent
The "alternative component" is the sum of each amount of the "loans sold with recourse" risk portfolio by level of recourse (as a percent of quarter-end total assets) times its alternative factor. The alternative factor for loans sold with recourse of less than 6% is equal to the weighted average recourse percent on such loans. A credit union must compute the weighted average recourse percent for its loans sold with recourse of less than six percent (6%). Substitute for corresponding standard component if smaller.
